What is Cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrencies are digital or virtual currencies that use cryptography for security and operate on decentralized networks called blockchains.

What is Risk Level?

Risk levels categorize cryptocurrencies based on their market stability, price fluctuations, and reliability.

What is ROI (Return on Investment)?

ROI measures the profitability of an investment, calculated as a percentage increase or decrease from the original investment amount.

What is Sentiment Analysis?

Sentiment analysis uses algorithms to determine whether news and social media reactions to cryptocurrencies are positive, neutral, or negative.

What is the Fear-Greed Index?

The Fear-Greed Index measures the emotions driving the cryptocurrency market, ranging from extreme fear to extreme greed.
